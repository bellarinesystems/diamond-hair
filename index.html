What We Offer

Services Tailored to You

Every service at Diamond Hair is delivered with care, expertise, and premium products.

Cut & Style

A tailored cut and finish that suits your face shape, lifestyle, and personal style. Tara takes the time to consult with you so your hair turns out exactly how you want it — shiny, fresh, and easy to manage at home.

Colour & Highlights

From full colour transformations to subtle highlights, Tara uses premium products that deliver vibrant, long-lasting results. Your hair stays healthy and luminous well beyond your appointment.

Blowdry & Finish

Perfect for a special event or just because you deserve it. A professional blowdry using quality styling products that leave your hair looking salon-fresh and feeling incredible.

Hair Treatments

Nourishing treatments designed to restore, strengthen, and add serious shine to your hair. Tara selects the right treatment for your hair type using the beautiful products Diamond Hair is known for.

Wedding & Event Styling

Your big day deserves hair that makes you feel extraordinary. Tara works closely with you in the lead-up to create a style that holds beautifully and photographs even better.
